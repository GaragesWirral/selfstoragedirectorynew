Storage Unit Size Guide

25 sq ft (5×5)

Ideal for small number of boxes, small furniture items, and seasonal items. Similar to a large closet.

50 sq ft (5×10)

Perfect for contents of a small flat or studio. Can fit a queen mattress, dresser, TV and several boxes.

75 sq ft (7.5×10)

Suitable for a 1-bedroom flat, including a sofa, bed, dining set, and several boxes.

100 sq ft (10×10)

Accommodates contents of a 2-bedroom flat, including appliances, living room furniture, and bedroom sets.

150 sq ft (10×15)

Fits contents of a small house, including major appliances, several furniture sets, and many boxes.

200+ sq ft (10×20 or larger)

Suitable for contents of a 3-4 bedroom house, including all furniture, appliances, and household items.
