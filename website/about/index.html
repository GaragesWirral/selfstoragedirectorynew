About Storage Finder

The UK's leading independent self storage comparison and directory service, connecting customers with the perfect storage solutions since 2010.

Our Mission

To simplify the process of finding and comparing self storage facilities across the UK, helping individuals and businesses make informed decisions based on their unique storage requirements.

Our Story

Storage Finder was established in 2010 by a team of industry experts who recognized the challenges people faced when searching for reliable self storage options. What began as a small directory covering just a few regions has evolved into the UK's most comprehensive self storage resource.

Over the years, we've built relationships with thousands of storage providers across the country, from national chains to independent local businesses. Our platform now covers over 2,500 facilities across 79 regions and hundreds of cities throughout the UK.

We take pride in our independence, ensuring that all listings and information are presented fairly and without bias. Our goal is to empower customers with the knowledge and resources needed to make the best choice for their specific storage requirements.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Directory: Access to thousands of self storage facilities across the UK.
  • Detailed Information: Find specifics about facility features, security measures, access hours, and pricing.
  • Easy Comparison: Compare multiple facilities side-by-side to find the best match for your needs.
  • Local Focus: Detailed coverage of storage options by region and city to help you find convenient solutions.
  • Expert Advice: Resource guides and tips for selecting, packing, and maximizing your storage experience.
  • Verified Reviews: Real customer feedback to help guide your decision-making process.
